Meet Ivy

Hi, I’m Ivy Sloan — a voice actor based in Portland, OR. I specialize in audiobook narration, commercial voiceovers, and character work for games and animation. Whether it is a heartfelt memoir or a whimsical sidekick, I love helping stories come alive through voice.

With a background in theater and a lifelong love of storytelling, I bring emotional depth and vocal range to every project. I record from a professionally treated home studio, using high-quality gear and fast turnaround times to keep your production moving smoothly.

When I’m not in the booth, you’ll find me sipping strong coffee, exploring local bookstores, or studying dialects for my next role. I believe that a good voice performance is all about connection — to the story, the listener, and the moment.
